# Find Unique Number XOR

ID: 44_find_unique_number_xor
Level: Level 09 - Bitwise

## Goal
Every number appears twice except one. Find the unique number.

## Expected Format
- Input: First line: n. Second line: n integers.
- Output: One integer: the unique number.

## Sample
Input:
```text
7
4 2 5 2 4 9 5
```

Output:
```text
9
```

## Skills
bitwise XOR

## Extra Detail
The input should have exactly one value that appears once.
