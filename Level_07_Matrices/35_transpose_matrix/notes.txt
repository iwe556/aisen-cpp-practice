# Transpose Matrix

ID: 35_transpose_matrix
Level: Level 07 - Matrices

## Goal
Print the transpose of a matrix, where rows become columns.

## Expected Format
- Input: First line: rows and columns. Next rows: matrix values.
- Output: The transposed matrix.

## Sample
Input:
```text
2 3
1 2 3
4 5 6
```

Output:
```text
1 4
2 5
3 6
```

## Skills
row-column swap

## Extra Detail
The output has original column count as its row count.
