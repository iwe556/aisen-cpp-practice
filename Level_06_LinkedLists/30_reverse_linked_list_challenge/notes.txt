# Reverse Linked List Challenge

ID: 30_reverse_linked_list_challenge
Level: Level 06 - Linked Lists

## Goal
Reverse the direction of a linked list so the tail becomes the head.

## Expected Format
- Input: A linked list, according to the prompt format.
- Output: The linked list after reversal.

## Sample
Input:
```text
Example: 10 -> 20 -> 30
```

Output:
```text
30 -> 20 -> 10
```
