# Count Special Numbers

Problem ID: 01_count_special_numbers
Level: Level 01 - Basics

## Problem Statement
Given n numbers, count how many are divisible by 3 or 5.

## Input
Input will be in the form of a "vector<int>" and be passed through the function parameter

## Output
Will output "n numbers are divisible by 3 or 5"

## Skills
Loops, if, modulus
