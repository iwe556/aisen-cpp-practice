# Count Special Numbers

ID: 01_count_special_numbers
Level: Level 01 - Basics

## Goal
Read n, then n integers. Count how many values are divisible by 3 or by 5.

## Expected Format
- Input: First line: n. Second line: n space-separated integers.
- Output: One integer: the count of special numbers.

## Sample
Input:
```text
5
12 7 9 20 15
```

Output:
```text
3
```

## Skills
Loops, conditions, modulus

## Extra Detail
A number divisible by both 3 and 5 still counts once.
