# Sum of Digits

ID: 02_sum_of_digits
Level: Level 01 - Basics

## Goal
Read one positive integer and print the sum of its digits.

## Expected Format
- Input: One positive integer.
- Output: One integer: the digit sum.

## Sample
Input:
```text
4729
```

Output:
```text
22
```

## Skills
while loop, %, /

## Extra Detail
Check what should happen for a one-digit number.
